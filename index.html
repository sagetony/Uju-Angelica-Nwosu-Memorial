Uju Angelica Nwosu

This memorial website was created in memory of our loved one, Uju Egwuogwu nee Nwosu, 48 years old, born on March 20, 1974, and passed away on July 30, 2022. We will remember her forever.

Pharmacist Obianuju died at the intensive care unit of Jos University Teaching Hospital at the age of 49years on the 30th day of July 2022 from breast cancer complications. She is survived by her husband barrister Anthony Egwuogwu, five surviving siblings and two children.

She carried out her pharmaceutical project work on pharmacognocy which explains her firm belief in crude drugs. During her days of battle with cancer, she completely rejected orthodox method of cancer treatment which is primarily the use of chemotherapy but rather opted for use of herbs which did not seem to have pronounced effect on the cancer cells. Until her sleep, she held on tight to her belief even in so much pains; but is now free from such pains and agony of this world. May her soul continue to rest in peace until we meet to depart no more
